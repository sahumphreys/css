Benjamin Britten

"The greatest musician I have ever worked with" (Sir Charles Mackerras)

Timeline

1913 - Born in Lowestoft

1921 - Starts formal piano lessons, theory lessons with Ethel Astle

1923 - Begins viola lessons with Audry Alston

1930 - Scholarship to Royal Academy of Music

1934 - Meets Peter Pears; A Boy is Born broadcast on BBC

1939 - Leaves for America

1976 - Dies in Aldeburgh
