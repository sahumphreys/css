Late Runners

Taking up running in the second half of life can be challenging but immensely rewarding. Late Runners is committed to supporting others taking up that challenge.

It's really not about how fast you go. Let's face it, we're not going to compete against those in their 20s or 30s. Nor is it about how far. Of course we can take on a marathon distance or even ultra-marathons but running just a few km each week will be transformative for mind and body.

In these pages you will find some tips for getting started and, more importantly, how to keep going.
